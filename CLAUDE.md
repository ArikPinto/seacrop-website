# SeaCrop Brand Guide — CLAUDE.md

## Company Overview
**SeaCrop** is a deep-tech company harvesting marine microbial plankton using electrostatic fibre technology.
- **Tagline:** Small particles. Global impact.
- **Mission:** Create access to marine microbial plankton as an unlimited resource for life on Earth.
- **Vision:** A world where SeaCrop resources fully support global nutritional security, transforming food production into a force that restores balance to our planet.
- **Creative Direction:** Visionary Precision

## Typography

### Primary Font — Garet
- Classification: Geometric Sans Serif
- Use: Logo wordmark, headlines
- Weights: 11 weights (Thin → Black) + Italics; available as variable font
- Character: High x-height, clean soft letterforms, smooth masculine tone, closed oval counters

### Secondary Font — Montserrat
- Use: Body copy, paragraphs, all running text
- Weights available: Thin, ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Black
- Both fonts are available on Google Fonts

### Hierarchy Guidance
- Headlines/display: Garet Bold or ExtraBold
- Subheadings: Montserrat SemiBold or Bold
- Body: Montserrat Regular or Light
- Captions/labels: Montserrat Medium, uppercase tracking for labels

## Logo

### Design
"SEACROP" in wide-tracked capital letters. The **"O" is replaced by a stylised icon** with curved, overlapping sections that simultaneously represent:
- A crop field viewed from above (sectioned plots)
- A leaf unfurling (growth and life)
- Ocean current movement (flowing lines)

Teal/aqua tones for the ocean/sustainability meaning; darker green section for the agricultural/growth meaning.

## Tone of Voice

SeaCrop's voice is **determined, scientific, and human** — never corporate or cold.

1. **Visionary and future-focused** — speak with long-term perspective; inspire belief in lasting solutions
2. **Ingenious and solutions-driven** — pragmatic, inventive, undeterred; turn challenges into opportunities
3. **Bold, yet sincere** — confident and deliberate; honest about real progress, never empty promises
4. **Human and relatable** — approachable and empathetic; translate complex science for real people

### Brand Values
- **Determined** — laser focus, relentless pursuit, unwavering commitment
- **Pioneering** — bold experimentation, courageous thinking, challenging limits
- **Resilient** — adapts, recovers, grows stronger through challenges
- **Learning** — curious, humble, continuously evolving

### Sample Language Patterns
- Use: "the smallest particles", "microscopic innovation", "planetary ambition"
- Favor: active voice, short punchy sentences for headers, richer sentences for body
- Avoid: passive, vague, overly technical jargon without context
- Hashtags: #SeaCrop #OceanInnovation #FutureOfFood #FoodSecurity #OceanTech

## Visual Language

- **Photography:** Ocean environments, microscopy/science, sustainability themes — rich, deep tones
- **Design feel:** High-contrast, modern, tech-forward with natural ocean inspiration
- **Motion/pattern:** Wave-like, flowing forms referencing plankton drift and ocean currents
- **Layout:** Clean, precise — "visionary precision" is the creative direction

## Key Facts for Copy
- Technology: Positively charged electrostatic fibres attract plankton (negatively charged) without harming the ecosystem
- Impact: Phytoplankton = ~50% of world's primary productivity; up to 100 billion organisms/litre of seawater
- Problem: 2+ billion people face food insecurity; traditional agriculture strains ecosystems
- Co-founders: Itai Katz (PhD physicist, Technion University) + Shahar (practical execution, early experiments) + Arik Pinto (operations)
- Application areas: Aquaculture, livestock feed, human nutrition, ocean restoration (eutrophic waters / dead zones)
